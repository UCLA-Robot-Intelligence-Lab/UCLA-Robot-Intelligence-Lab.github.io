People

Yuchen Cui

Yuchen is the founder of this Lab. Yuchen's research interest lies at the intersection of machine learning, robotics, and human-robot interaction. Her work focuses on developing algorithms, frameworks, and systems that facilitate efficient robot learning from human interactions. Specifically, she investigates how to develop efficient learning algorithms, harvest untapped data modalities, and design intuitive interfaces for interactive learning. Her ultimate goal is to democratize autonomation by making robots accessible to everyday users.

Raayan Dhar

Raayan is interested in machine learning and deep learning broadly. He is especially interested in robotics, generative models, vision, and reinforcement learning. He is currently working on improving diffusion policies.

Tracey Tay

Tracey is passionate about the intersection of AI and robotics, with a particular focus on advancing intelligent systems. Her experience includes exploring innovative applications of natural language processing (NLP) and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG). She has also delved into computational robotics, developing skills in formulating robotic systems and behaviors as mathematical problems for analysis and optimization. These experiences inspire her to experiment with robotic arms, aiming to integrate intelligent decision-making and control. Through this work, she seeks to bridge theoretical foundations with practical implementations, driving progress in the synergy between AI and robotics. Aside from academics, she enjoys being outdoors, doing a mixture of sports, music, cooking and getting to know people :)

William Jiang

William is an undergraduate student pursuing a degree in Computer Science and Engineering at UCLA. His work centers on robot policy learning using gaze data from Meta's Aria glasses and innovative applications in computer vision. In addition to his academic pursuits, William designed and developed this website. He is also a member of UCLA's Division II hockey team.
